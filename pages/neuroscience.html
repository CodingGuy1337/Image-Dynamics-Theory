Neuroscience lens
From latent dynamics to measurable brain dynamics.

Read IDT as dynamical systems: cognition is movement on a manifold shaped by prediction error, control signals, and relational consistency constraints.

Attractors and metastability

If Z evolves by relaxing an energy functional, stable mental states correspond to attractors. Flexible cognition corresponds to metastable trajectories hovering near multiple basins.

Abrupt “state changes” in experience map onto basin-to-basin transitions in the latent landscape.

Predictive processing mapping

Energy terms resemble a predictive processing story: sensory mismatch (per vs x_t), predictive mismatch (sem vs x_{t+1}), and policy mismatch (act vs y).

Testable hooks

  • Higher liminality ↔ increased entropy / faster reconfiguration (shorter dwell time, higher complexity metrics).
  • Strong axis ↔ shared stable subspace across tasks (manifold overlap).
  • Goal revision ↔ outcome-driven changes in control coupling (policy representation shift).

Reflection in the brain

Reflection is internal re-encoding of transitions: situation → action → outcome → updated priors/goals. Look for replay, consolidation, and post-error policy adjustments.
